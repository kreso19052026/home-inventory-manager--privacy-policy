Privacy Policy for Home Inventory Manager Last updated: 03.07.2026 Home Inventory Manager ("the app") is developed by Kozo Technology ("we", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses and how it is used. 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT The app stores the inventory data you create (item names, descriptions, photos, receipts, categories, and related notes) locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store this data on any remote server, and we do not have access to it. 2. PERMISSIONS USED BY THE APP - Camera: used to take photos of your items and to scan barcodes for product lookup. Photos are stored locally on your device. - Photo/Media access: used to let you attach existing photos from your gallery to inventory items. - Internet access: used only to look up product information when you scan a barcode (e.g. product name/description from a public barcode database). No personal or inventory data is sent as part of this lookup. - Notifications: used to remind you about warranty expirations and scheduled reminders you set within the app. 3. DATA SHARING We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or inventory information with third parties. Data you choose to export (e.g. PDF reports) is generated on your device and shared only through the sharing method you explicitly select (email, messaging apps, etc.). 4. DATA STORAGE & SECURITY All inventory data is stored locally in the app's private storage on your device. Uninstalling the app removes this data. We recommend using the app's export feature to back up important records before uninstalling or switching devices. 5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES The app may display ads served through Google AdMob, which may collect data as described in Google's own privacy policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy). We do not control this data collection directly. 6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY This app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. 7. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. 8.
